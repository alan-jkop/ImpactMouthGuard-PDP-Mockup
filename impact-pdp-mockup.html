Product Details

The Donuts custom mouthguard from Impact Mouthguards features a vibrant donut-themed design on a medical-grade custom-fitted guard. Each guard is fabricated from a precise dental impression of your teeth for a secure, comfortable fit that won't compromise speech or breathing.

Made from BPA-free, FDA-approved materials right here in the USA, this guard provides professional-level impact protection for athletes of all ages.

Shipping & Returns

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I take my dental impression?▼
We send you a free, easy-to-use impression kit with full instructions. Simply mix the putty, bite down for 3 minutes, and mail it back using the prepaid label.
How long does it take to receive my mouthguard?▼
Standard orders take 10–14 business days after we receive your impression. Expedited 3-day lab service is available as an add-on.
Can I reorder using my existing impression?▼
Yes! Select "Yes" under the reorder option and we'll use your impression on file.
Is the mouthguard safe for all sports?▼
Absolutely. Our custom guards are designed for high-impact sports including football, hockey, boxing, MMA, lacrosse, and more.
